Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

  • help_outline What is Brozerly for?

    Brozerly is a social network designed to ask for financial help in your network of contacts in the application, something similar to crowdfunding, but private and thought for personal needs.

    In our profiles of Facebook, Twitter, Google Plus , we usually share examples of use cases of Brozerly

    In this video you can see an example of use of Brozelry.

  • help_outline What is an SOS and how do I ask for help in Brozerly?

    The way to ask for help in Brozerly is to launch an SOS which indicates the requested amount, the description (here is where you explain why do you need the monew) and the deadline for contributions. When an SOS is launched, your contacts in Brozerly will get a notification to contribute to your SOS if they wish.

    In Brozerly it is also possible to launch a public SOS so that all users in Brozerly can contribute. You can also share your SOS in other networks to encourage others who do not have a Brozerly account to contribute to your SOS.

  • help_outline What do objects are for?

    Favourite objects at Brozerly are designed so that you can share with your contacts your needs and passions.

    It can be from your pet to your mobile phone or your studies. If your contacts know your needs and passions, they can better understand why it is so important for you to receive help for any of your SOS.

  • help_outline How do I turn off notifications?

    Within the navigation bar click on the settings icon (the wheel) and then on settings.

    Once you access the settings page you have several buttons available to activate or deactivate notifications by email or in the device from which you are using the application (mobile or web). In addition to language settings, you can also link your Google or Facebook accounts with your Brozerly account.
