The first social app for financial help and personal needs

Free and no fees !

Brozerly allows you to ask for help in your network of friends so that your contacts can help you with your personal needs.


 Connect

Friendship is the ability to count on each other when you need it the most



1. Create your network

Create your network of friends in Brozerly. Invite your friends to Brozerly or request to connect with other members in Brozerly.


2. Ask for help

Launch an SOS to ask for help, you decide if you want to launch a private or public SOS (open all members in Brozerly).


3. Exchange

Exchange with your network private messages or comments to answer questions about your SOS.


4. Collect

The members of your network who wish will make economic donations to your SOS, this is the way they help you. You will receive the total amount in your bank account.

